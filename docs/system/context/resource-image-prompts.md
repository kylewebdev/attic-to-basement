# Image Prompts — Attic to Basement Estate Liquidators

Prompts for generating imagery across the entire website. All images should follow the brand aesthetic: warm natural lighting, real-feeling, earthy tones (warm whites, muted sage, soft wood tones, honey floors), no stock photo feel. Think editorial lifestyle photography — calming, grounded, and human.

**Important:** No text, words, signs, price tags, labels, logos, or branding in any image. AI generators struggle with text — keep every image purely visual.

**Format notes:**
- Hero/header images: 16:9
- Square/portrait sections (Turn, Our Promise founder): 1:1 or 4:5
- Card thumbnails (appraisal categories, services): 3:2
- Resource article headers: 16:9
