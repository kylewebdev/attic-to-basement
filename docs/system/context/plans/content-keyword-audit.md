# Content Keyword Audit — "Estate Sales" Focus

## Goal

Ensure the existing site content is optimized for the primary keyword "estate sales" without disrupting the current brand voice and messaging strategy.

**Primary keyword:** "estate sales"
**Constraint:** Homepage uses a story-driven narrative — preserve the emotional messaging. Changes should be subtle, not a rewrite.

## Opportunities

### Homepage (light touch)

The homepage narrative shouldn't be rewritten, but there are places to weave "estate sales" in naturally without breaking the story flow. Potential approaches:

- Add a brief descriptor line or subheading near the Hook that grounds the page (e.g., a tagline beneath the H1)
- Work "estate sales" into the Turn or Process section body text where we describe what ABE actually does
- The Process section describes the estate sale process step-by-step without naming it — this is the most natural place to add the keyword

### Reviews Page

- H1 "What Families Are Saying" could become "What Families Are Saying About Our Estate Sales" or similar

### Contact Page

- H1 "Let Us Take It From Here" could add a subtitle mentioning estate sales
